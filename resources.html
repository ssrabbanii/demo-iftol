RESOURCES FOR IFTOL 2022

The following sections will cover the resources on details of online lessons, “hackathon” and development workshops for finalists’ teams.

Online Training Courses

Participants will be given a hybrid of online live stream sessions via zoom, and a series of video lessons distributed through GoSkills channel of YouTube and bilibili. The lessons contain the aspects of fintech, and some pitching skills to equip students with the knowledge and technique to put their ideas on paper and present to others, and the judges, through FTOL.

Hackathon

After the online training courses, a hackathon will be given to the participating students, providing a chance to exchange their thoughts and designs, with the purpose to inspire their minds and improve their final products.

Development workshop for finalists’ teams

The shortlisted teams are going to be announced on 12th February 2022. To further enhance their minds on fintech and skills in presenting, a development workshop for finalists’ teams will be presented in the mid of February.

Micro-consulting day

A chance for finalists to improve on their projects and interact with other contestan

  • Brainstorming
  • Communicate/connect with like-minded people who are interested in similar areas of technology
  • They are all motivated and driven
  • Can form a community with them and exchange knowledge.
